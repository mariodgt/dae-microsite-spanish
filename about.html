WE'RE AN EXCHANGE COMPANY,
WE JUST DON'T ACT LIKE ONE

It's time for a new source for exchange. One that's less about fees and more about a personalized approach to getting travelers going. In an industry full of fees, barriers and unpleasant surprises, DAE is changing the rules. Our innovative have-a-week, get-a-week model does away with trading power, points and everything else that keeps travelers from booking the exchanges they want. It's fast, it's easy and it works.

NO MEMBERSHIP FEES

Most exchange companies charge membership fees. At DAE, Membership is free and our exchange fee is among the lowest in the industry. You don't pay a thing until we've successfully matched you to your destination. We think that's how it should be. If you travel more than once per year, we encourage you.

YOU'RE NOT MARRIED TO
YOUR EXCHANGE COMPANY

It doesn't matter what exchange company you're a member of, you can still deposit your week with us. DAE Membership has no obligations and offers more options with less hassle. It's just the way we do things.

SEARCH ONLINE AND SEE THE WORLD OR MAKE A REQUEST AND WE'LL FIND IT

Some people prefer to do their own research. With DAE, you aren't required to bank a week and we don't charge Members for access to our website and inventory. Our goal is to give you the tools you need to find the destination you want. Your online Member Dashboard provides access to our inventory, and every week deposited is reserved solely for our Members. Our exChange Agents are constantly searching to secure inventory for custom requests; they take the time to get to know your vacation needs and work tirelessly to make a match. And when you do exchange, our fees are among the lowest in the industry. That's just another way we're changing exchange.

DAE is all about enhancing the vacation ownership lifestyle for our Members. Gain more access, connect with other Owners and Members, and keep up with the latest and greatest through DAE partners.

DAE WORKS WITH DEVELOPERS, HOA'S AND RESELLERS TO MAKE EXCHANGE MORE AFFORDABLE AND EASIER TO ACCESS

At DAE, we realize that it's our job to support you and promote the health of your timeshare or membership product. The best way we can do that is to ensure that your Owners or Members are traveling and using the vacation time they purchased. Through consumer-centric benefits, lower fees and powerful rewards, DAE is focused on the experience that today's owners need. Discover how DAE is driving a return on innovation.
